Duvance Account Deletion

Last updated: 2026-03-31

Delete Your Account In the App

You can permanently delete your Duvance account and related app data from inside the app.

Steps:

  1. Open Duvance and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to the Profile screen.
  3. Open the side menu.
  4. Tap Delete My Account.
  5. Confirm permanent deletion.

What Is Deleted

When you delete your account, the app deletes your account record and related in-app data such as portfolio data, transaction history, achievements, and other profile-linked records stored for the app experience.

What May Be Retained

Certain legal records, security logs, fraud-prevention records, or aggregated analytics may remain for a limited period on service provider systems where required for legitimate operational or legal reasons.

Need Help?

If you cannot access the app and need help with deletion, contact duvanceapp@gmail.com.
